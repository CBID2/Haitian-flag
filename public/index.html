L'Union fait la force: History of the Haitian Flag

1964-1986

During François "Papa Doc" Duvalier's presidency, the colors red and black were used to convey the message of unity amongst the Haitian people and independence from the French.

1986-Present

Ten days after the departure of François Duvalier in 1986, the flag's colors were restored to the original red and blue, which have remained unchanged to the present day.
